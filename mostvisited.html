IT Kiosk

IT Kiosk Information
The IT Kiosk is an extension of the ITMS Service Desk and provides a physical space where CDU staff and students can obtain face-to-face support for CDU IT and Communications services. We provide students with communication technologies support, and staff with technology support in teaching and learning spaces. Our expertise includes the support of CDU's VoIP telephones, video conferencing, Wifi networks and connectivity, PDA's, smartphones and web-based applications.

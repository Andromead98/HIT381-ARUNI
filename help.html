ARUNI Help

On this screen you can find tips and tricks on how to use ARUNI

To zoom out on the Map, use your thumb and forefinger to pinch the map.

To zoom in on the Map, use your thumb and forefinger and push apart.

To select a building on the Map, use your finger and tap an Icon or Building Name.

To view more information about a building, use your finger and tap on a button.

To view further building information, tap on the 'View Building Info' after selecting a button.

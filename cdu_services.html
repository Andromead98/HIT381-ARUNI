IT Kiosk

Red 1.1.20

The IT Kiosk is an extension of the ITMS Service Desk and provides a physical space where CDU staff and students can obtain face-to-face support for CDU IT and Communications services. We provide students with communication technologies support, and staff with technology support in teaching and learning spaces. Our expertise includes the support of CDU's VoIP telephones, video conferencing, Wifi networks and connectivity, PDA's, smartphones and web-based applications.
Childcare Centre

Green 3

The CDU childcare centre is tucked away on campus and provides support to the community and staff and students of CDU. You can trust that while you are working or studying, we will be taking the best care of your little one. We have indoor and outdoor play areas, hands on activities and a team passionate about children. Call the Centre Manager to have a chat today.
Post Office

Red 2

Australia Post has a shop on Charles Darwin University's Casuarina campus. At the on-campus post office you can pay many of your bills (including phone, PowerWater, Darwin City Council rates and dog registration), recharge your mobile and calling cards, pick up your favourite magazines and choose a gift for any occasion from our extensive range. The post office also provides PO Boxes and is an agent for the Commonwealth Bank.
Menzies

Red 9

Menzies School of Health Research is one of Australia’s leading medical research institutes. We are dedicated to improving the health and wellbeing of Indigenous Australians, and are global leaders in tropical research in to life-threatening illnesses.
CYCM

Oragne 6.1

The Centre for Youth Music (CYCM) teaches music in a manner that is fun, educational and tailored to each student's individual needs and goals. The CYCM offers music curriculum for students of all ages starting from the "very young" in the "Junior Music Program" right through to the "young at heart" adults in our "Non-Award Programs". Catering for the beginner to the more advanced student, the CYCM provides instrumental, vocal and theory tuition as well as offering a diverse and exciting range of ensemble programs. At the CYCM, students work with the Northern Territory's top music professionals and leading visiting musicians, who are committed to sharing their musical knowledge and experience by offering accessible and affordable music instruction and performance opportunities for all in our community.

Chinese Garden

Near Yellow 1

Charles Darwin University's Chinese Garden is a symbol of the University's interaction with the broader community and an educational, ornamental and recreational resource for the wider community to enjoy. The Chinese Garden is also available for private and community hire.
Indonesian Garden

CDU shaded area

The Indonesian Garden was designed to celebrate and continue to forge a collaborative friendship between CDU and the Indonesian community in the Northern Territory and Indonesia. The garden is a tranquil space which is ideal for relaxation and contemplation. The Indonesian-inspired wooden hut is an ideal meeting place or stage for small performances, talks, private events or gatherings. The garden is connected to a shaded area, attached to the University, so you can enjoy both outdoor manicured lawns and covered seated spaces.
